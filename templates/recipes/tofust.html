Tofu Stir-Fry

Ingredients

  • 1 block tofu, diced
  • Assorted vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, mushrooms)
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice for serving

Directions

  1. Press tofu to remove excess water, then dice into cubes.
  2. In a wok or large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Add tofu cubes and cook until golden brown on all sides. Remove from pan and set aside.
  4. Add remaining oil to the pan and saut√© garlic and ginger until fragrant.
  5. Add assorted vegetables and stir-fry until tender-crisp.
  6. Return tofu to the pan, add soy sauce, and stir-fry for another minute.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot over cooked rice.
