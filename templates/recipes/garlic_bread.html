Cheesy Garlic Bread

Ingredients

  • French bread
  • Butter
  • Garlic powder
  • Garlic cloves
  • Italian seasoning
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parsley

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Cut your French bread loaf lengthwise and place on top of a baking sheet.
  2. In a small bowl combine softened butter, garlic powder, minced garlic, Italian seasoning and shredded cheese.
  3. Spread evenly on each side of the garlic bread. Top with Colby and mozzarella cheese.
  4. Bake in the oven for 12-15 minutes until butter is melted and bubbly.
