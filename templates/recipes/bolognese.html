Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 celery stalk, diced
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked spaghetti for serving

Directions

  1. In a large skillet, brown ground beef over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion, minced garlic, diced carrot, and diced celery. Cook until vegetables are softened.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, beef broth, tomato paste, and dried oregano.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Simmer for 20-30 minutes.
  5. Serve hot over cooked spaghetti.
