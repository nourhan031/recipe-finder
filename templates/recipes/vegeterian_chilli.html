Vegetarian Chili

Ingredients

  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) corn kernels, drained
  • 1 can (15 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion, bell pepper, and garlic. Cook until softened.
  3. Add chili powder and cumin. Cook for another minute.
  4. Add diced tomatoes, black beans, kidney beans, and corn.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Simmer for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with chopped cilantro or shredded cheese.
