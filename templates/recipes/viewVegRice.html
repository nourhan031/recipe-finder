Vegetable Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided
  • 6 cups of diced vegetables of choice
  • 3 large eggs, beaten
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper
  • 3 cups of cooked and cooled rice
  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced scallions, white and light green parts

Directions

  1. cook the veggies
  2. scramble the eggs
  3. stir fry the rice
  4. put the vegetables and eggs to wok with the rice for about 3 minutes.
  5. sprinkle the scallions over the top and serve right away.
