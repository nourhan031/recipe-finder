Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • assorted vegetables
  • soy sauce
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • sesame oil
  • cornstarch
  • salt
  • pepper.

Directions

  1. Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces and season with salt, pepper, and cornstarch.
  2. In a hot pan or wok, add sesame oil and cook chicken until browned.
  3. Remove chicken from the pan.
  4. In the same pan, add more oil if needed and sauté minced garlic and ginger.
  5. Add vegetables and stir-fry until tender.
  6. Return chicken to the pan, add soy sauce, and cook until everything is heated through.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot over cooked rice.
