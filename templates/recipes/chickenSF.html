Chicken Stir-Fry

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • assorted vegetables
  • soy sauce
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • sesame oil
  • cornstarch
  • salt
  • pepper.

Directions
Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces and season with salt, pepper, and cornstarch. In a hot pan or wok, add sesame oil and cook chicken until browned. Remove chicken from the pan. In the same pan, add more oil if needed and saut√© minced garlic and ginger. Add vegetables and stir-fry until tender. Return chicken to the pan, add soy sauce, and cook until everything is heated through.
