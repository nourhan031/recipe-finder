Lemon Garlic Chicken

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, juiced and zested
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Directions

  1. Season chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and dried oregano.
  2. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant.
  4. Add chicken breasts and cook until golden brown on both sides and cooked through.
  5. Remove chicken from the pan and set aside.
  6. Deglaze the pan with lemon juice, scraping up any browned bits.
  7. Return chicken to the pan, add lemon zest, and simmer for a few minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.
